How it works

From your first click to your first day at work, we handle the heavy lifting so you can focus on finding the right family.

1
Day 1

Online Application

Fill out our digital form. Tell us about your experience, your skills (like cooking or childcare), and what kind of family you'd like to work with. No paperwork required.

2
Day 2-3

Video Interview

Speak with one of our recruitment specialists. We get to know your personality and verify your references. This helps us ensure we match you with families who respect your boundaries.

3
Week 1

Peasy Academy Prep

Complete a free half-day orientation. We walk you through your rights, our "Dignity Charter," and give you a few tips to stand out during family interviews.

4
Week 2

Smart Matching

Our algorithm suggests 3-5 families that fit your preferences perfectly. You choose who you want to interview with. You are always in control of the decision.

5
Week 3+

Placement & Support

Once matched, we handle all the contract processing and insurance setup. You start your new job with a dedicated support manager available to you 24/7.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about joining Peasy.

Are there really no fees?

Correct. Peasy is 100% free for helpers. We charge the employers for our matching and HR management software. You never pay us a cent.

What is the Dignity Charter?

It's a mandatory agreement every family signs. It guarantees a private room, clear working hours, set rest days, and respectful communication.

What if I don't like the family?

We offer a 30-day "Right Match" guarantee. If things aren't working out within the first month, we will help you find a new placement immediately.

Do you help with visas?

Yes. We have an in-house documentation team that manages all visa processing, renewals, and legal paperwork for both HK and Singapore.
